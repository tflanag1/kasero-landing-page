Why I Created Kasero

"Years ago, while shopping at my local market in Santiago, Chile, a kind vendor began calling me 'Casero' - not tourist, not foreigner, but someone who belonged. 'Casero' means homemade, familiar, and welcome; it captures that beautiful feeling of being embraced as family, of finding home in unexpected places. As international educators, we know this truth deeply: when living overseas, your friends become your family. Kasero exists to create this same warmth among teaching colleagues around the world - a community where we're never strangers, always family."

Tom Flanagan
Kasero Founder & International Educator
